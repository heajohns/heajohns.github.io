Cait's Roses

I recently saw my cousin, Caitlin. She and I have always been best friends even when we don't see each other or talk as often as we would like. This year we are both in our senior year in college. She is a harpist in Oberlin, a music conservatory, and has a senior recital she will be playing in May.

Over the past two years, I have been learning how to bake and decorate cakes. Unfortunately, I am not the best at making flowers out of gumpaste. One of my good friends in college, now my cake mentor, taught me how to make roses. I have now frozen them to keep them until needed. This is the first step in a beautiful cake for one of my most favorite of people.
