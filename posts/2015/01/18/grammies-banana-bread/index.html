Grammie's Banana Bread

It is the weekend before my last semester of my undergraduate career at George Mason University and I am kind of freaking out. I have no job propositions at the moment; nonetheless, I have faith that I will be fine. To reassure myself I decided I wanted to bake, so I went with a favorite: banana bread. Growing up I always made my grandma's banana bread. This time I decided to stay safe and make the same recipe. There is just something about this type of banana bread that I enjoy more than others. Here is the recipe:

1 1/4 cup sugar
1 1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda

Mix the dry ingredients and then continue to the wet ingredients.

1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
3 very ripe bananas

Mix together with beaters until smooth. Then in a large bowl, mix both wet and dry ingredients together in a non-metallic bowl with a wooden spoon and bake at 350 degress for 50-60 minutes.
